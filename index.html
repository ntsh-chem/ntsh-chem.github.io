PROCEEDINGS OF THE SHEVCHENKO SCIENTIFIC SOCIETY

ISSN: 2708-2350

The Proceedings of T. Shevchenko Scientific Society, Chemical Sciences publish original outputs of the national and international scientific communities focussing on fundamental, applied and interdisciplinary aspects of modern chemical sciences in the form of reports of experimental findings or review articles. The Proceedings have been published since 1897. During 1897-1939 the journal was published as Proceedings for Mathematical, Natural Science and Medical Section of T. Shevchenko Scientific Society and was the first Ukrainian Scientific Journal in natural sciences and mathematics. The Journal was reinstated in 1997. This resulted in the establishment of the section of Chemical Sciences and achieving the status of the subject recognition in line with the requirements for publication of results of doctoral level studies. All publications are subject of peer-review process. The Proceedings of T. Shevchenko Scientific Society have been included in the Index Copernicus International Journals Master List since 2013. The journal is included in the list of compulsory circulation of scientific publications in the Ukraine and is sent to the V. I. Vernadsky National Library and Libraries of all Higher Education Institutions and those of the Ukrainian National Academy of Sciences.
