TO THE ANNIVERSARY OF PROFESSOR DUTKA VOLODYMYR STEPANOVYCH


The article is dedicated to the 70th anniversary of Professor of Physical and Colloid Chemistry Chair, Doctor of Chemical Sciences Volodymyr Stepanovych Dutka. A brief biographical description, the main milestones of official activity, directions of scientific and educational work are given. The public activity of Professor Dutka V.S. is especially noted in the Shevchenko Scientific Society and in the training and education of young chemists – members of the Small Academy of Sciences.
